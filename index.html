Hello, we're Vanshika Gifts

What started as a small passion project — wrapping heartfelt gifts for friends and family — grew into something much more beautiful. We were founded on the simple belief that a gift should be more than just an object.

Based in Surendranagar, Gujarat, we handcraft custom gifts for weddings, birthdays, festivals, baby showers, and corporate events. Every basket we weave, every hamper we curate is made with the recipient in mind.

🌿 Natural & Sustainable

We use natural materials — bamboo, dried botanicals, handmade paper.

💝 Made to Order

Nothing is mass-produced. Every gift is crafted fresh, especially for you.

🎨 Fully Customisable

Colours, themes, contents, messages — we personalise every detail.

✦ Quality Guaranteed

If you're not delighted, we'll make it right — always.
